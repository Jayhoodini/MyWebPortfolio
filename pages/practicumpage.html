The ESGDash system plays a vital role in supporting environmental monitoring and ESG reporting within the organization by offering automated data processing, user-friendly visual dashboards, and standardized reporting tools. These features help stakeholders make informed decisions and comply with regulatory standards. Throughout the development process, the student undertook a wide range of responsibilities.
